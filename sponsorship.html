Sponsorship is the ultimate act of welcome—and anyone can do it.

Over the last year, hundreds of thousands of everyday Americans have stepped up to provide a bridge to safety and a path to opportunities for families seeking refuge in the United States through ground-breaking government programs. Sponsors are guides, neighbors, friends, and cheerleaders for newly arriving families seeking to rebuild their lives in the U.S.
